Here is a comprehensive design system and UI concept.

1. Color Palette: "Deep Slate Luxe"

This palette focuses on depth, using subtle tone variations rather than pure black to prevent eye strain.

Base Background: `#292b3e` (Neutral Color) - Main background.

Primary Accent: `#6d5dfc` (Primary Color) - For primary CTAs, active states, and highlights.

Secondary Accent: `#00e5b4` (Secondary Color) - For growth indicators, positive KPIs, and success states.

Tertiary Accent: `#ff5c5c` (Tertiary Color) - For negative indicators and alerts.

Additional Contextual Colors from the Document:

Card Background: `#31344a` (Slightly lighter slate) - For cards, forms, and elevated elements.

Text (Primary/Headings): `#ffffff` (Pure White).

Text (Secondary/Body): `#a0a3b8` (Muted Blue-Gray).

Text (Disabled/Subtle): `#64677f` (Darker Gray) - For placeholders and borders.

Input Fields Background: `#222433` (darker than base).

Borders/Grid Lines: `#3c3f58`.

2. Typography

Font Family: `inter` (Headline, Body, and Label Font).

Headings: `inter`, white (`#ffffff`).

Body: `inter`, Muted Blue-Gray (`#a0a3b8`).

3. UI Components & Styling

Cards: Rounded corners (`roundedness: 2` translates to moderate rounding, around 8-12px as suggested) with a very subtle border (`#3c3f58`) instead of shadows.

Buttons:

Primary: Solid `#6d5dfc` with a slight outer glow on hover.

Secondary: Ghost button (transparent with white text) or outline with `#a0a3b8` border.

Input Fields: Background `#222433` (darker than base) with `#a0a3b8` text and white placeholder, border color `#3c3f58`.

Icons: Thin line icons (feather icons) in `#a0a3b8` or solid white.

4. Layout & Scene Example (Hero Section)

Background: Solid `#292b3e`.

Navigation: Top nav transparent with white text, using a subtle border (`#3c3f58`) at the bottom.

Hero Text: "Revolutionize Your Workflow." in White.

Subtitle: "The premium SaaS solution for advanced analytics." in `#a0a3b8`.

CTA Button: Solid `#6d5dfc`.

Dashboard Preview: A glowing, high-contrast dashboard mockup placed slightly below the fold to show interactivity.

5. Data Visualization (Charts/KPIs)

Lines/Bars: `#00e5b4` (Secondary Color - Mint) for positive trends, `#6d5dfc` (Primary Color - Purple) for main data, and `#ff5c5c` (Tertiary Color - Red) for negative indicators.

Grid Lines: Muted dark gray (`#3c3f58`).

This design emphasizes professionalism, trust, and advanced functionality, often used in B2B SaaS, and is highly effective at reducing eye strain during long-term use. The overall `color_mode` is `dark`, `spacing` is `2` for a normal layout.
